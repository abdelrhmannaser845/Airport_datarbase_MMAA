# Airport Management Project(MMAA)

---

## Description

This repository contains the database for an airport management system, with a deliberate focus on modeling and organizing airport-related data. The project emphasizes clarity, structure, and relevance to real-world airport operations.

---
 
## Features

- Employee Management
- Passenger Management
- Flight Management
- Aircraft & Maintenance Management
- Airline Management
- Hanger & Ground Facility Management
- Transportation Management
- Ticket & Booking System
- Weather Monitoring
